How to Play

Overview

Stellar Virtue is a cooperative game where players command virtuous AI fleets to defend human colonies from rogue AI ships. Survive 9 days to win!

Setup

The board has 12 sectors around Earth. Player bases are in sectors 1, 5, and 9. Enemy ships spawn in sectors 4, 8, and 12. Each player controls one or more of the 12 saint-themed ships.

Daily Office (7 Turns per Day)

  • Lauds – Morning prayer; gain 1 virtue point
  • Prime – Draw a Catholic Action Card
  • Terce – Move or attack
  • Sext – Charge or repair
  • None – Pray or use a virtue ability
  • Vespers – Move or attack
  • Compline – Rest; heal 1 health per ship

Actions

  • Move – Move a ship to an adjacent sector
  • Attack – Deal damage equal to charge level, then reset charge
  • Charge – Increase charge by 1
  • Repair – Heal 1 health on your ship
  • Pray – Answer a virtue question to grow in virtue

Theological Virtues (Received)

Faith, Hope, and Charity are supernatural virtues — gifts infused by God, not earned by effort. They grow through grace on Sundays and through fidelity to prayer.

  • Faith – Level 3 grants +1 max charge
  • Hope – Level 3 grants +1 max health
  • Charity – Level 3 allows prayer to heal allies

Cardinal Virtues (Acquired)

Prudence, Justice, Fortitude, and Temperance are natural virtues acquired through practice and good moral choices. Grow them by answering virtue questions during prayer and making greater-good moral choices during attacks.

  • Prudence (~8 sub-virtues) – Master to preview enemy actions
  • Justice (~13 sub-virtues) – Master for +1 attack damage
  • Fortitude (~6 sub-virtues) – Master to reduce damage taken
  • Temperance (~8 sub-virtues) – Master to keep charge after attacking

Each sub-virtue needs 2 correct answers to master. Master all sub-virtues of a cardinal virtue to unlock its combat bonus.

Moral Choices (Attacks)

When you attack during Terce or Vespers, you face a moral scenario with two options:

  • Lesser Good – Your attack deals normal damage
  • Greater Good – Bonus damage (+1) and progress toward a cardinal sub-virtue

Both choices succeed — the greater good simply earns more reward.

Gifts of the Holy Spirit

Seven gifts that perfect the virtues, received when theological virtues reach level 3: Wisdom, Understanding, Counsel, Fortitude, Knowledge, Piety, and Fear of the Lord.

Fruits of the Holy Spirit

Twelve fruits manifest as passive bonuses when virtues and gifts combine: Charity, Joy, Peace, Patience, Kindness, Goodness, Generosity, Gentleness, Faithfulness, Modesty, Self-Control, Chastity.

Grace & Fidelity

Grace grows through Sunday reflections and prayer. Fidelity measures your responsiveness to grace — pray regularly to keep it high. High fidelity helps theological virtues grow faster on Sundays. Merit is earned by cooperating with grace through good choices.

Sundays

On Sundays (Lord's Days), all ships rest and heal +2 HP. A reflection on grace is shown, and one theological virtue receives growth. No player or enemy actions — true Sabbath rest.

Winning

Survive 9 days without losing a base. Bonus victory for defeating all 24 enemy ships!
